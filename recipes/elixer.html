Elixer of Power and Endurance

Long fortold by the fathers of our fathers, and the fathers of their fathers and so on through the ages, there exists an elixer which brings man power and endurance previously afforded only to Odin himself. To manifest this elixer, the noble warrior will require the following magical objects:

The Magical Objects

  • Eagle Feather
  • Honey of Freya
  • Four draughts of mead
  • Musical Quartet
  • Warrior's Courage

The Process of Creation

  1. Consume the four draughts of mead
  2. Summon the courage of a true warrior
  3. Initiate the enchantment of the musical quartet
  4. Trace the feather upon the object of thine affection
  5. Lap up the honey of Freya with thine own tongue
